Fried Tacos

Fried Tacos are delicious and a hit if you have tim to make them

On some social media sites the are called "Arizona Tacos"

    Ingredients
  • 8 (5-inch) corn tortillas
  • 1 pound ground sirloin
  • 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt, divided
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup minced white onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • lime wedges, for serving
  • 1/4 cup salsa
    Cooking instructions
  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Lay 8 tortillas flat on a surface
  3. combine beef,taco seasoning, and 1/2 teaspoon salt in a medium bowl.
  4. press 1/4 cup of beef mixture onto half of each tortilla
  5. Heat 1/4 inch of oil in a high sided skillet over medium heat until shimmering. Working in batches, carefully add taco at a time, flat, to the hot oil.
  6. Hold empty side of the tortilla with a pair of tongs, and fold over beef filling once the shell is softened, about 5 seconds.
  7. Cook turning once until shell is golden brown and beef is no longer pink in the center, 30 seconds to 1 minute per side. An instant thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F.
  8. Transfer to a paper towel lined plate, and sprinkle with remaining 1/4 teaspoon of salt.
  9. Serve immediately topped with onion and cilantro. Serve with lime wedges, salsa, and hot sauce.
